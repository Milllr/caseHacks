The why behind cuts

What we chose not to build.

Every other team will pitch one of these. We rejected each one with a reason — because shipping in 18 months means picking exactly one wedge and cutting the rest.

Not a chatbot.
Wealthsimple already has one. The differentiator is structural, not conversational.
Not a Scotia Smart Investor reskin.
It is not in MoneySense's 2026 Best Robo-Advisors list. Polishing the wrong product loses.
Not a TikTok / finfluencer play.
82% of 18–24s already use social for investing info. Bank cannot credibly enter the channel CIRO is warning about.
Not crypto in iTRADE.
Wealthsimple Crypto is CIRO-registered. Big Six are structurally excluded. Not Scotia's lane.
Not a Scene+ tie-in.
Loyalty bolt-ons are cross-sell theater. They dilute Scene+'s everyday-spend hook without moving FHSA activation.
Not "all 18-to-34s."
Building for everyone is building for no one. We picked Segment B — Capital-Constrained Curious — and defended it with market math.

The moat

Aggregation is not origination.
The mortgage is the moat.

Wealthsimple Households can see Maxine's FHSA balance. They cannot show her a Scotia mortgage pre-approval — because mortgages are originated, not aggregated. Twelve years in market and Wealthsimple still does not issue mortgages at scale. OSFI B-20 / B-21 requires a deposit funding base and capital they don't have.

Wealthsimple lacks
Scotia owns
Cannot originate the mortgage at year five. Wealthsimple Households shows the balance — it cannot underwrite the loan. 12 years in market, zero mortgage scale.
Third-largest Canadian residential mortgage originator. FHSA balance maps directly to downpayment, then to a Scotia mortgage.
Aggregates but does not own. Households shows the customer their accounts elsewhere; the actual underwriting, capital, OSFI relationship stays at Scotia.
First-party financial product origination — chequing, FHSA, mortgage, deposit-funded balance sheet. The relationship is the contract, not the dashboard.
No view of the customer's primary chequing account until Open Banking Phase 1 launches and the customer consents.
Real-time, first-party transaction-data visibility today. The pre-fill of the $1,920 tax-savings number is exclusively Scotia's — for now.
No branch network for the multilingual newcomer / affluent advisory hand-off.
National branch and multilingual advisor network — an optional upsell at month 12, never a gate.
